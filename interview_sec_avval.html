🎤 Interview with Avval Halani: "A Day Inside APJ Building"

👦 Q: How does your typical day in APJ start, Avval?

"Every morning, I 🚶‍♂️ walk into APJ through the Main Gate. It feels fresh and energetic, especially with all the greenery around! 🌳🌞"

🏛️ Q: Where do you head first?

"I go straight to the Financial Lab 📊. That's my zone! I set up my laptop 💻, grab a chair, and start my work — research, data crunching, and project planning!"

👨‍🏫 Q: What's next after setting up?

"After getting into the flow, I usually head upstairs to Prajish Sir's Office 🏢 to report my work or discuss any new updates. It's a quick check-in that keeps me on track. ✅"

☕ Q: Any chill breaks?

"Of course! Around mid-morning, I NEED my coffee! ☕ I dash to the cafeteria — it's my mini-reset before jumping back into work."

💼 Q: After coffee?

"I return to the Financial Lab 📊 and dive back into research. Sometimes, I take small walks around APJ — it helps me refresh my mind! 🧠✨"

🌇 Q: How does your day end?

"In the evening, I pack up 📦, say a quick bye to colleagues, and exit again through the Main Gate. 🚶‍♂️🌇 The evening breeze makes it a nice ending to a busy day."

🚻 Q: Anything you wish was better?

"Honestly, I visit the washrooms quite frequently 😅 and sometimes too crowded. Also, navigating the corridors can be a little confusing for newcomers — better signs would help!"
